How to play

Guess the football club from its crest in 6 attempts.

🛡️ The crest is shown blurred. It gets clearer with each wrong guess.
💡 A new hint is revealed after each wrong guess.
⏭️ Use Skip to pass — it costs one attempt.
♾️ Infinite mode — a new crest after every round.

Hints revealed in order:

🌍 Country 🏆 League 🎨 Colours 🏟 Stadium 📍 City
